# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Provide clear and actionable feedback.
- Assume good faith.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Threats, stalking, or unwanted attention.
- Sexual content or advocating harm.
- Disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers.

The maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including temporary or permanent removal from the project.
